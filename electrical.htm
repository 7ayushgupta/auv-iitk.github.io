Electrical Subsystem

The electrical subsystem is responsible for taking input from the various sensors like Pressure Sensors, IMU etc and actuating the thrusters and pneumatic systems using micro-controllers. We also have our self designed Battery Management System.

Highlights

Battery Management System
The battery management system of the Bot is responsible for easily charging the battery and maintaining the flow of current as well as making sure the flow and battery power are within feasible limits. The BMS also makes sure that battery never reaches a state where it may get damaged. The BMS also makes sure all the actuators, processors etc get exactly the right amount of voltage and current they need.
Circuit boards
Self designed and printed boards help the smooth actuation from output from microcontrollers. These boards are first tested on a ground bot with similar output motors and then installed on the main Bot so as to make sure that everything works seamlesskly.
