Mechanical Subsystem

Mechanical subsystem has 2 main tasks. To design a structure which provides minimum drag force in water and to water proof the entire structure. The mechanical also design the pneumatic assembly which is responsible for gettings tasks done.

Recent Innovations

Connectors
We use self designed and manufactured connectors, prototypes of which were 3D printed and final designs will be manufactured via CNC.
Bot Design
The recently manufactured Bot has minimal drag and ample testing space for pneumatic systems. The design is a modular so as to allow ample expansion and space for new and innovative ideas.
Thruster
The team recently added 4 new thrusters allowing for 6 degrees of freedom as well as faster and more accurate motion and turning.
