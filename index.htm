Autonomous Underwater Vehicle

Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur

As one the upcoming projects of IIT Kanpur we aim to create efficient underwater vehicles which can get a wide array of tasks done. Alongside a wide array of industrial applications our designs can be used by NIOT for furthering their research on Autonomous Underwater Vehicles.

Software
We have uploaded our entire code on Github.com. We use ROS to connect various systems and we use OpenCV for Image Processing. Our entire code is written in C/C++ and Embedded C. The code runs on Odroid processors and takes input from 2 cameras and processes them. Our well structured code is divided into various logical levels which are isolated from each other allowing us to efficiently apply Control and Intelligence algorithms.
Mechanical
We have manufactured our bot from Aluminium while the hull is made up of acrylic. We design all part on Solidworks and then simulate their strength and optimize them for least mass and optimum dimensions on Ansys. The model can be viewed publicly on Github.com. Actuated with 6 thrusters our bot has 5 degrees of freedom and a maximum velocity to 2 m/s.
Electronics
We give power to our thruster using 12V Li-ion battery duly regulated by our battery management system. We take input from a wide array of sensors including Pressure Sensors, IMU etc. Our custom printed circuit boards provide actuation to thrusters using Motor Drivers.
